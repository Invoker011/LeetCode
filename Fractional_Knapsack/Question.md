🧩 Problem: Fractional Knapsack

You are given:

n items
Each item has:
value[i] → profit of the item
weight[i] → weight of the item

You are also given a knapsack with capacity W.

📌 Task

You can take fractions of items (not just whole items).

👉 Maximize the total value you can carry in the knapsack.

🔢 Input
value[] = [60, 100, 120]
weight[] = [10, 20, 30]
W = 50
